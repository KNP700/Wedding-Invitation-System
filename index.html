Our Story

Samadhi and Harsha didn’t start off as a love story—they started off arguing.

Back in 2020, they were just classmates who didn’t really care about each other. When they had to work together, it quickly turned into constant debates. Harsha stayed calm and always had a response, which only annoyed Samadhi more.

But over time, the arguments turned into jokes, and the rivalry became a solid friendship. Somewhere along the way, Harsha caught feelings. Samadhi acted clueless… but maybe she wasn’t.

After a lot of teasing from friends, Harsha finally asked her out—and she said yes.

Three years later, they’re still together and decided it’s time to get married and keep arguing forever.
